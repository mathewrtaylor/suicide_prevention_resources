## ‚ö†Ô∏è EMERGENCY GUIDANCE FOR MODERATORS

**Immediate Danger:**
If a member indicates they are in immediate danger or have a specific plan:
1. Encourage them to call emergency services immediately (911, 112, 999, or local equivalent)
2. Suggest they go to the nearest emergency room
3. Ask if there's someone nearby who can stay with them
4. Provide the crisis hotline numbers above

**Active Listening Tips:**
- Take all mentions of suicide seriously
- Listen without judgment
- Don't promise confidentiality if safety is at risk
- Encourage professional help
- Follow up when appropriate

**What NOT to do:**
- Don't leave the person alone if possible
- Don't debate whether suicide is right or wrong
- Don't act shocked or judgmental
- Don't promise you can keep their thoughts a secret
