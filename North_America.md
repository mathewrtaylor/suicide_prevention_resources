## ðŸ‡ºðŸ‡¸ [United States](#-united-states)

**National Crisis Hotlines:**

**988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline**
- **Phone:** 988
- **Text:** Text "HELLO" to 741741
- **Chat:** 988lifeline.org/chat
- **Availability:** 24/7
- **Languages:** English, Spanish (press 2)
- **Website:** 988lifeline.org
- **Services:** Crisis counseling, suicide prevention, mental health support

**Crisis Text Line**
- **Text:** Text "HELLO" to 741741
- **Availability:** 24/7
- **Languages:** English
- **Website:** crisistextline.org
- **Services:** Free 24/7 text support

**Emergency Services:**
- **Phone:** 911

**Specialized Services:**

**The Trevor Project (LGBTQ+ Youth)**
- **Phone:** 1-866-488-7386
- **Text:** Text "START" to 678-678
- **Chat:** thetrevorproject.org/get-help
- **Availability:** 24/7
- **Services:** Crisis support for LGBTQ+ youth under 25

**Veterans Crisis Line**
- **Phone:** 988, then press 1 OR 1-800-273-8255, press 1
- **Text:** 838255
- **Chat:** veteranscrisisline.net/get-help/chat
- **Availability:** 24/7
- **Services:** Support for veterans, service members, National Guard, Reserve, and their families

**Trans Lifeline**
- **Phone:** 877-565-8860 (US), 877-330-6366 (Canada)
- **Availability:** Varies
- **Languages:** English
- **Services:** Peer support for transgender people

**National Domestic Violence Hotline**
- **Phone:** 1-800-799-7233
- **Text:** Text "START" to 88788
- **Availability:** 24/7
- **Services:** Support for domestic violence situations

**National Sexual Assault Hotline**
- **Phone:** 1-800-656-4673 (HOPE)
- **Availability:** 24/7
- **Services:** Support for sexual assault survivors

**Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA) National Helpline**
- **Phone:** 1-800-662-4357 (HELP)
- **Availability:** 24/7
- **Languages:** English, Spanish
- **Services:** Referrals for substance abuse and mental health treatment

**National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI) Helpline**
- **Phone:** 1-800-950-6264
- **Text:** Text "HELPLINE" to 62640
- **Availability:** Mon-Fri, 10 AM - 10 PM ET
- **Languages:** English
- **Services:** Mental health information, resources, referrals

**Disaster Distress Helpline**
- **Phone:** 1-800-985-5990
- **Text:** Text "TalkWithUs" to 66746
- **Availability:** 24/7
- **Languages:** English, Spanish
- **Services:** Crisis counseling for people experiencing emotional distress related to disasters
